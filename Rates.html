Rates

All the rooms in the Lodge are $50 per double room. A third person in the same room is an additional $15.


Parking is free, and there is plenty of room for trucks and trailers to be left at the lodge while you ride your ATV to the trail.


All rooms can be reserved in advance by calling (304) xxx-xxxx. A deposit of 50% per room in the lodge or $75 for the mobile home is required for advance reservations. This deposit will count toward the cost of your lodging. If you cancel, the deposit will be returned if you give us two weeks notice. The balance is due at check-in and is not refundable. This guarantees that you will have a room for your entire stay, and that we will not turn away other guests who could have been accommodated.


You can mail us a cashier's check or money order.
