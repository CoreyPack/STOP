Backpack Blessings

A program providing a weekend supply of nutritious food for children while school breakfast and lunch is unavailable. Each food bag contains, 2 breakfasts, 2 lunches, 2 snacks, 2 fruit and/or vegetables. All food is non-perishable and provided to children free of charge.


It is the hope of The STOP Coalition and our partners that this program will support the health and well-being of every student that participates.


This program is funded through various grants, community churches, and local businesses.
